EX-30R

We shipped a new kind of excavator mounted rockdrilling rig for tunneling purposes in a nothern mine in Finnish Lapland. It was specially made for Takeuchis TB295W, compatible with its quick connector and its hydraulic system. It will be utilised in the quarries equipment drilling purposes. Radius of operation: 350° Operation height: 10.5 metres in vertical direction, 8.5m horizontally Maximum depth of 2.4m with pit size of 51mm.
